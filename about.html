Our Story

Kerala's Most Trusted Travel Partner

Nature Green Holidays was founded with a simple mission: to make Kerala tourism accessible, affordable and unforgettable for every traveller. Based in Ernakulam — the commercial capital of Kerala — we are ideally positioned to serve visitors arriving at Cochin International Airport, Ernakulam Junction or Ernakulam Town railway stations.

Our team of local travel experts has an intimate knowledge of Kerala's geography, culture and hidden gems — from the mist-covered peaks of Munnar and Wayanad to the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey and the pristine beaches of Kovalam. We design every itinerary with care, ensuring you experience the real Kerala.

Whether you are planning a romantic honeymoon, a fun family holiday, a thrilling adventure trip or a peaceful pilgrim journey, we have the experience and the fleet to make it seamless.
