Package Overview

Celebrate your love story in God's Own Country with our signature Kerala Honeymoon Package. This carefully crafted 5-day, 4-night itinerary takes you through the misty tea gardens of Munnar, the serene backwaters of Alleppey and the wild jungles of Thekkady — all in luxurious comfort.

Your journey begins and ends at Ernakulam (Kochi), with a dedicated vehicle and experienced driver who ensures every transfer is smooth and stress-free. Accommodation is carefully selected — from boutique hill resorts in Munnar to a deluxe overnight houseboat on Vembanad Lake.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival in Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Arrive at Cochin International Airport / Ernakulam Railway Station. Your driver will be waiting at the arrival gate. Transfer to Munnar — en route, enjoy the scenic Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Check into your resort; evening leisure at Munnar town. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 02

Munnar Sightseeing — Tea Gardens & National Park

Full day Munnar sightseeing: Eravikulam National Park (home of the Nilgiri Tahr), Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point and Kundala Lake. Stroll through endless tea plantations at TATA Tea Museum. Romantic evening at Top Station with panoramic views of the Western Ghats. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Munnar → Thekkady (90 km, ~3 hrs)

After breakfast, transfer to Thekkady. Check into your spice plantation resort. Afternoon visit to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary — enjoy a 2-hour boat ride on Periyar Lake watching elephants, bison and deer. Evening spice plantation walk and Kathakali dance performance. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 04

Thekkady → Alleppey Houseboat (150 km, ~4 hrs)

Morning transfer to Alleppey (Alappuzha). Board your private deluxe houseboat at 12 noon. Cruise through tranquil backwater canals, paddy fields and coconut groves on Vembanad Lake. All meals served on board — freshly prepared Kerala cuisine. Overnight on the houseboat under the stars.

Day 05

Alleppey → Kochi Departure (55 km, ~1.5 hrs)

Morning cruise on the backwaters; check out of houseboat at 09:00. Transfer to Ernakulam / Cochin Airport for your onward journey. Trip ends with wonderful memories of God's Own Country!
