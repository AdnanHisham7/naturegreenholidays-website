Package Overview

Five nights, four destinations, one unforgettable state. Our Complete Kerala package adds an extra night in the historic city of Kochi to the classic circuit — giving you time to explore Fort Kochi's Chinese fishing nets, colonial spice markets and vibrant art galleries before heading home.

Perfect for those who want to experience Kerala from the mountains to the sea without feeling rushed. Your private vehicle and driver are with you every step of the way, and all accommodation is hand-picked for comfort and location.

Route

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival in Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Pickup from Cochin Airport or Ernakulam Station. Drive to Munnar with waterfall stops en route. Check in to your resort; evening leisure in Munnar town. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 02

Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Kundala Lake and TATA Tea Museum. Sunset at Top Station. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Munnar → Thekkady (90 km, ~3 hrs)

Transfer to Thekkady after breakfast. Afternoon Periyar Lake boat ride; evening spice plantation walk and Kathakali performance. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 04

Thekkady → Alleppey Houseboat (150 km, ~4 hrs)

Morning transfer to Alleppey. Board houseboat at noon; full afternoon and evening cruising Vembanad Lake with all Kerala meals on board. Overnight on houseboat.

Day 05

Alleppey → Kochi (55 km, ~1.5 hrs)

Check out houseboat at 9:00 AM. Drive to Kochi; visit Fort Kochi — Chinese fishing nets, St Francis Church, Mattancherry Dutch Palace and the spice market. Check in to Kochi hotel. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 06

Kochi Departure

Breakfast and check-out. Free morning for any last sightseeing or shopping in Ernakulam. Transfer to Cochin Airport or Ernakulam Station. Safe travels!
