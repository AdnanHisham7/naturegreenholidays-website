Package Overview

Cross state lines and discover the cultural contrasts of South India. This 8-night itinerary combines Kerala's natural beauty with Tamil Nadu's temple grandeur — from the Meenakshi Amman Temple of Madurai and the sacred shores of Rameswaram to the sunrise point of Kanyakumari.

Starting in Kochi and ending in Trivandrum, this is a popular route for travellers who want to fly in via one airport and out via another. It's also an excellent choice for history enthusiasts and temple-circuit travellers.

8 Nights / 9 Days

Duration

2–8 Persons

Route

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival Kochi — Fort Kochi Sightseeing

Pickup and Fort Kochi sightseeing — Chinese fishing nets, Dutch Palace, spice market. Check in to Kochi hotel. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 02

Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Drive to Munnar via Cheeyappara waterfall. Check in; evening leisure. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Top Station, TATA Tea Museum. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 04

Munnar → Thekkady (90 km, ~3 hrs)

Transfer after breakfast; Periyar Lake boat safari; spice plantation and Kathakali performance. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 05

Thekkady → Madurai (140 km, ~3.5 hrs)

Cross into Tamil Nadu and arrive at the temple city of Madurai. Visit the iconic Meenakshi Amman Temple and Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace. Evening at Gandhi Museum. Overnight in Madurai.

Day 06

Madurai → Rameswaram (170 km, ~4 hrs)

Drive to Rameswaram island. Visit the magnificent Ramanathaswamy Temple corridor (world's longest temple corridor), Agni Theertham and Dhanushkodi. Overnight in Rameswaram.

Day 07

Rameswaram → Kanyakumari (320 km, ~6 hrs)

Drive to Kanyakumari. Arrive in time for sunset at the three-sea confluence. Visit Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Kumari Amman Temple. Overnight in Kanyakumari.

Day 08

Kanyakumari → Trivandrum (90 km, ~2.5 hrs)

Drive to Trivandrum. Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Napier Museum. Evening at leisure in the capital. Overnight in Trivandrum.

Day 09

Trivandrum Departure

Breakfast; check-out and transfer to Trivandrum Airport or Railway Station. Tour concludes.
