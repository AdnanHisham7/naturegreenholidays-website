Package Overview

Journey from the backwaters to the very tip of India. This 7-night grand tour adds an exciting Kanyakumari excursion to the classic Kerala circuit — stand at the confluence of three seas and watch one of India's most spectacular sunrises at the southernmost point of the subcontinent.

Two nights in Trivandrum allow you to explore the capital at leisure and make a full day trip to Kanyakumari without rushing. The package ends with drop at Trivandrum, ideal for onward travel by air or train.

Route

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival Kochi — Fort Kochi Sightseeing

Pickup from Kochi. Explore Fort Kochi's heritage — Chinese fishing nets, spice warehouses, St Francis Church. Check in to Kochi hotel. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 02

Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Drive to Munnar via Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Check in and evening at Munnar town. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Kundala Lake, Top Station. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 04

Munnar → Thekkady (90 km, ~3 hrs)

Transfer to Thekkady after breakfast. Periyar Lake boat safari; evening spice plantation walk and Kathakali performance. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 05

Thekkady → Alleppey Houseboat (150 km, ~4 hrs)

Board houseboat at noon; full afternoon cruising Vembanad Lake; all meals on board. Overnight on houseboat.

Day 06

Alleppey → Trivandrum (155 km, ~3.5 hrs)

Check out houseboat at 9:00 AM. Drive to Trivandrum. Visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple and Kovalam beach. Check in. Overnight in Trivandrum.

Day 07

Trivandrum → Kanyakumari Day Trip (90 km, ~2.5 hrs each way)

Full day excursion to Kanyakumari — the southernmost tip of India. Visit Vivekananda Rock Memorial (ferry required), Thiruvalluvar Statue, Kumari Amman Temple and the Gandhi Memorial. Return to Trivandrum hotel. Overnight in Trivandrum.

Day 08

Trivandrum Departure

Breakfast and check-out. Transfer to Trivandrum Airport or Railway Station. Tour concludes.
