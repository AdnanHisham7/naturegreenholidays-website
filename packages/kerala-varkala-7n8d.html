Package Overview

Add the dramatic red cliffs and golden sands of Varkala to the classic Kerala itinerary. This 7-night package swaps the second Trivandrum night for a day at Varkala — Kerala's most stunning beach destination — before finishing in the capital.

Varkala's cliff-top promenade, ancient Janardana Swami Temple and natural mineral spring make it unlike any other Kerala beach. It's a perfect addition for beach lovers and those looking for a slightly off-the-beaten-track experience. Drop at Trivandrum.

7 Nights / 8 Days

Duration

2–8 Persons

Route

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival Kochi — Fort Kochi Sightseeing

Pickup from Kochi Airport/Station. Fort Kochi heritage walk — Chinese fishing nets, Dutch Palace, spice market. Check in to Kochi hotel. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 02

Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Drive to Munnar via waterfalls. Check in to resort; evening in Munnar town. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, Top Station, TATA Tea Museum. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 04

Munnar → Thekkady (90 km, ~3 hrs)

Transfer to Thekkady; Periyar Lake boat safari; spice plantation and Kathakali evening. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 05

Thekkady → Alleppey Houseboat (150 km, ~4 hrs)

Board houseboat at noon; backwater cruise with all meals on board. Overnight on houseboat.

Day 06

Alleppey → Varkala (115 km, ~3 hrs)

Check out houseboat at 9:00 AM. Drive to Varkala. Check in to cliff-top resort. Spend the afternoon exploring the beach, Janardana Swami Temple and the famous natural spring. Sunset along the cliff promenade. Overnight in Varkala.

Day 07

Varkala → Trivandrum (50 km, ~1.5 hrs)

Morning leisure at Varkala beach. Check out and drive to Trivandrum. Afternoon: visit Padmanabhaswamy Temple (exterior) and Napier Museum. Evening at leisure. Overnight in Trivandrum.

Day 08

Trivandrum Departure

Breakfast and check-out. Transfer to Trivandrum Airport or Railway Station. Tour ends.
