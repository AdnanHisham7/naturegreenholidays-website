Package Overview

Short on time but big on experiences? Our 3-night, 4-day package is the ideal quick Kerala escape, packing in two of the state's most iconic destinations — the misty tea gardens of Munnar and the legendary backwaters of Alleppey. Both worlds, one seamless journey.

Your trip begins with pickup from Ernakulam and ends with a drop at Cochin. A private AC vehicle and experienced driver are with you throughout, handling all transfers so you can simply sit back and enjoy the scenery.

Route

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 01

Arrival in Kochi → Munnar (130 km, ~4 hrs)

Pickup from Cochin International Airport or Ernakulam Railway Station. Transfer to Munnar — en route, stop at the scenic Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls. Check into your hill resort and enjoy a leisurely evening at Munnar town. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 02

Full Day Munnar Sightseeing

Explore the best of Munnar — visit Eravikulam National Park (home of the Nilgiri Tahr), Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point and Kundala Lake. Stroll through the TATA Tea Museum and taste freshly-rolled teas. End the day at Top Station for sweeping Western Ghats views. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 03

Munnar → Alleppey Houseboat (170 km, ~5 hrs)

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Alleppey (Alappuzha). Board your private deluxe houseboat at noon. Cruise through tranquil backwater canals, paddy fields and coconut-fringed villages on Vembanad Lake. Freshly prepared Kerala meals served on board. Overnight on the houseboat.

Day 04

Alleppey → Kochi Drop (55 km, ~1.5 hrs)

Wake up to a peaceful morning cruise on the backwaters. Check out from houseboat at 9:00 AM. Transfer to Ernakulam Railway Station or Cochin Airport. Your Kerala adventure ends here — see you again!
