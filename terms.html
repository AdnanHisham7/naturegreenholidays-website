3. Pricing & Payments

All prices are quoted in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of GST unless otherwise stated. Prices are subject to change without prior notice until a booking is confirmed.

Accepted payment methods: Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/IMPS bank transfer, debit/credit card. We do not accept cryptocurrency.

For package tours, a minimum advance of 25–30% of the total package cost is required to confirm the booking. The remaining balance is due 48 hours before the start of the tour, or as specified at time of booking.

Taxi and vehicle bookings require confirmation payment (amount communicated at booking time). Day-of-travel bookings may require full payment upfront.

4. Cancellation Policy

The following cancellation charges apply to confirmed package tour bookings:

  • More than 15 days before departure: Full refund minus a ₹500 processing fee per person.
  • 8–14 days before departure: 25% of total package cost is non-refundable.
  • 4–7 days before departure: 50% of total package cost is non-refundable.
  • 1–3 days before departure: 75% of total package cost is non-refundable.
  • No-show or cancellation on the day of departure: 100% of total package cost is non-refundable.

For taxi and vehicle bookings: Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. No-shows are non-refundable.

Hotel bookings within packages are subject to the individual hotel's cancellation policy, which may differ from the above. We will communicate specific hotel policies at time of booking.
